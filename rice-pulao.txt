Ingredients:
- 1 medium sized potato
- 1 medium sized onion
- 1 cup rice
- 2 teaspoon oil
- 1/3 teaspoon cummin seeds
- 1/4 teaspoon mustard seeds
- 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
- 1/5 teaspoon chili powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 3 teaspoon tomato sauce
- 2 bay leaves
- 10-15 black pepper
- little cinnamon
- water

Directions:
1. Soak the rice in water for 15 minutes
2. Chop potato and onion
3. Put the oil in cooker and heat it on medium flame for 5 minutes
4. Add mustard seeds in the oil and wait till they start popping
5. Now add onion and cummin seeds, and wait till onion become little brown
6. Now add turmeric powder, chili powder, bay leaves, black pepper, cinnamon, and salt, and stir for 2-3 minutes
7. Now add sauce and potato to this mixture, and stir for 2 minutes
8. Now add rice and 2 cups of water
9. Further add salt to your taste
10. Close the lid of the cooker and cook the rice pulao on full flame for 5 cooker whistles
